About Agrass Drone Development

About Us

Agrass Drones Development is a business with the passion for improving agriculture in rural area through the use of drones. We are located at Ayomso in the Ahafo Region close to Ayomso R/C Basic School

Vision

To become the leading provider of agricultural drone spraying services in the region and across Ghana, ensuring safer and more efficient farming practices for rural farmers.

Mision

To revolutionize agriculture by offering farmers an affordable and safe spraying service using drone technology, reducing their exposure to harmful chemicals while increasing productivity and efficiency.

About the Innovation

This innovation was born out of the realization that many rural farmers struggle with health risks due to the chemicals they use in their agricultural activities. Despite their hard work to produce food, their well-being is often compromised by direct exposure to harmful pesticides and fertilizers. Recognizing this challenge, the founder set out to develop an agricultural drone service that would handle spraying tasks for farmers, eliminating their direct contact with hazardous chemicals. The service operates on a simple modelâ€”farmers can request drone spraying whenever needed, paying for the service rather than investing in expensive technology themselves. This approach makes advanced agricultural solutions accessible to rural farmers, improving their health, productivity, and overall agricultural efficiency. With the ambition to scale from the local community to the entire nation, this initiative aims to transform the way farming is done in Ghana, making agriculture safer, more sustainable, and technologically driven.
